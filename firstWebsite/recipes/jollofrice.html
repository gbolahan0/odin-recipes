Jollof rice

Description

Jollof rice is a popular dish from West Africa. its is a typical dish made with long grain rice, tomatoes, onions and other spices. In different countries in West-Africa there are many variations.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Plum tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomato paste
  • chicken stock
  • onions
  • vegetable oil
  • seasoning cubes
  • salt
  • thyme

Steps

  1. Blend the plum tomatoes, bell pepper, and onions until smooth.
  2. In a suacepan add the vegetable oil and heat.
  3. Into the pan add the blended sauce and cook for 10 minutes
  4. To the sauce add the tomato paste, seasoning cubes and chicken stock, stir and let cook for 12 minutes
  5. To the saucepan, add rinsed long grain white rice to the saucepan and stir. Add water to cover the rice and cook over medium-low heat for 10 to 15 minutes.
  6. You may need to check the rice, add an additional cup of water, and stir every few minutes to prevent burning.
Tips
  • Avoid over stirring, jollof rice burns when its stirred too much during cooking
