Puff puff recipe

Description

Puff-puff, as it is called in Nigeria, is a traditional African snack made of fried dough. Puff-puffs are made of dough containing flour, yeast, sugar, butter, salt and water, which is then deep fried in vegetable oil to a golden brown color. After frying, puff puffs can be rolled in sugar.

Ingredients

  • 200g of Flour
  • 15g of Yeast
  • 80g of sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • Warm water
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix salt, water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Set it aside for 5 minutes
  2. Add flour and mix with a wooden spoon
  3. Cover the mixture and set it aside to rise for approximately 1-2 hours
  4. Into a large saucepan, Pour in the vegetable oil and heat (low heat).
  5. When the oil is hot enough, use a spoon or spatula to dish up the batter and drop it in the oil. In a ball shape.
