Moin-Moin

Description

Moin-Moin is a steamed or boiled bean pudding made from washed and peeled black-eyed beans, combined with onions, eggs and fresh ground red peppers. It's a food that is a staple in Nigeria

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • onions
  • Fresh pepper
  • Tomato puree
  • Groudnut oil
  • water
  • Corned beef
  • Eggs
  • Wrapping leaves

Steps

  1. Soak the beans in cold water and remove the skin
  2. Grind the skinned beans, fresh pepper and onions together.
  3. Pour it into a moving bowl and mix well with a wooden spoon until light and creamy.
  4. Add tomato puree and mix well again, and add warm oil, the corned beef and diced eggs.
  5. Place about 3/4 spoonfuls of the mixture into the wrapping leaves and wrap firmly.
  6. Arrange into saucepan containing bed of leaves, add water and stream for an hour.
